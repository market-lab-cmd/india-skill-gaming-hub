What this homepage is built to do

Reduce the same mistakes before they become identity

Experienced players know that improvement rarely starts with a brilliant move. It starts with finding the repeated leak, naming it cleanly, and fixing it before it becomes your normal habit.

Give readers a real study path, not a random pile of advice

The strongest order is usually fundamentals, mistakes, decisions, awareness, then pattern work and risk. That is the order real sessions usually break down, and it is the order solid review usually rebuilds them.

Keep every lesson close to live play

Each page is built around the same teaching loop: what the spot asked for, what players usually misread, and what adjustment gives you a better next session.

Study Library

Choose the layer of play you want to sharpen

Use the library the same way an experienced player uses review notes: start with the leak that is costing you most often, then move one layer deeper once the base starts holding.
